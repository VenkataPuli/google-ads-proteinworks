06

Bidding Strategy

Based on real supplement sector CPC data (Google Ads Benchmarks 2025), a phased approach is recommended. The supplements sector requires at least 30 conversions before Smart Bidding performs reliably.

Weeks 1–2 · Phase 1
Maximise Clicks
Max CPC caps: £0.70 branded / £1.50 generic. Goal: gather impression and click data without overspending.
Weeks 3–4 · Phase 2
Maximise Conversions
Remove max CPC caps once first purchases are recorded. Monitor CPA closely.
Weeks 5–6+ · Phase 3
Target ROAS — 350–400%
Requires 30+ conversions for algorithm to perform reliably. Lower target initially to allow learning. Avg. order value estimated at £35–£55 based on 1kg/2kg bag prices.
